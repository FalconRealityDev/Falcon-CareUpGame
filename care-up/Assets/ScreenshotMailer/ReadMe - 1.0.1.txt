ScreenshotMailer mails your taken screenshots instantly to your own mail address.

- To configure ScreenshotMailer, go to Window/ScreenshotMailer Settings.
- To capture (and mail) a screenshot, simply call the static method ScreenshotMailer.CaptureScreenshot();. It requires no prior initialization.
