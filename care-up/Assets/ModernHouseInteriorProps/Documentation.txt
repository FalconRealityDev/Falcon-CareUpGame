Modern House Interior Props

A collection of assets that can be used to set dress a modern house environment. 

Demo scene: The demo scene has the props set dressing a lit house environment to show how everything looks together.

Props will either have custom textures to add extra details, or they will use one or more materials assigned to different portions of the props to switch out with Universal materials that are using tiling textures.

285 Prefabs
435 PBR Textures
608 Tiling 1k Textures
1 Water Shader
